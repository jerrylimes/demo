Google Blocks Racing Ads, Rancher Sentenced & Rodeo Mom Tragedy

Google Blocks Horse Racing Affiliate Ads Across U.S. Market

Google has updated its U.S. gambling policy to ban horse racing affiliate and comparison-site ads, allowing only licensed operators to promote their own products while shutting down third-party referral marketing across search, display, and video platforms.

Arlington Horse Ranch Owner Sentenced to 15+ Years in Prison

Robert Blessing, former Arlington horse ranch owner, was sentenced to over 15 years in prison for sexually abusing three underage victims over more than a decade, with the court also imposing community custody, fines, and fees while honoring the victims’ bravery in coming forward.

Rodeo Community Mourns the Loss of Beloved “Rodeo Mom” Shryl Cooper

Shryl Cooper, cherished wife of world champion Jimmie Cooper and mother to three accomplished rodeo athletes, died at 70 following unexpected medical complications after back surgery, leaving her family and the broader rodeo community mourning the loss of a woman known as a welcoming “rodeo mom” to many.

Britain Deploys Equine Heart Monitors to Track Thoroughbred Race Performance

British racing is using new heart-monitor technology on select Thoroughbreds to gather ECG data during training and races, aiming to uncover why rare exercise-associated cardiac deaths occur and eventually develop AI-assisted screening tools to prevent them.

Only 7,000 of These Giant Horses Remain & Five Live in South Carolina

U.S. Air Force veteran Stacy Pearsall cares for and helps conserve rare European Brabant draft horses on her South Carolina farm, finding peace in training them while also continuing her work to support and amplify the voices of fellow veterans.

Thailand Triumphs FEI Eventing Asian Championship & Limaye Claims Emotional Gold

Thailand clinched team gold and India’s Ashish Limaye earned an emotional individual victory at the FEI Eventing Asian Championship 2025, where intense competition, standout cross-country performances, and narrow margins defined an unforgettable weekend at Pattaya’s Thai Polo Club.
